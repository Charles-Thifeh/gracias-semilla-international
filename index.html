A Warm Welcome to

Gracias Semilla International Nursery and Primary School

We set out on a mission to create a day nursery in which children between 2 months old and 5 years of age would be able to learn and grow in confidence. Bright Start also offers wraparound care for children up to the age of 8, so we can continue to nurture your children during the first few years of their school journey.

Studies have shown that children do better when they are encouraged to learn using play as a method of teaching… and we wanted to expand and explore this concept to help each of our little cherubs reach their full potential.

read more

A Day Nursery Celebrating Curious Minds

We teach young minds that being curious is a wonderful thing. They have so many exciting life adventures to look forward to – and we want to prepare them as best we can. We encourage each child in our care to explore the world in the way they know best, with their imaginations!

It is our aim that each and every child leaves us improved. Whether that be in terms of speech, social awareness, problem solving – or any other area where your child is struggling. We are here to actively encourage learning through good fun… no matter the stage of learning they are at.

Of course, here at Bright Start Day Nursery, we want each of our children to feel included. That's why we support equal opportunities for all!

📖

Social & Fun

Nursery is a great place to start to learn social skills, and at Bright Start we make this a fun experience for every child. Our children can form these early friendship bonds in our variety of play areas.

🏠

Safe Enviroment

We have very high security standards and all staff are fully vetted and checked to ensure the safety of the children. Safety and security begins at the front door and continues throughout our nursery.

👩‍🏫

Caring Staff

At Bright Start our staff are passionate about their work and dedicated to the children in their care. We have chosen over qualified staff to ensure the very best care for the children and their development.

🧩

Learning Through Play

Through play, children develop problem-solving skills, imagination and creativity, language and observation skills, and memory and concentration. This is at the core of our ethos at Bright Start.
